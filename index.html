About

A studio for designers, architects and collectors.

Founded by Toby Revis in 2001, the studio specialises in custom carpets for residential and commercial interiors. We work with architects, interior designers and private clients — for fine homes, hotels and luxury retail.

Toby studied painting and architecture at Syracuse University and in Madrid, qualified as a gemologist at Van Cleef & Arpels, and was a senior buyer at Tiffany & Co. for the Elsa Peretti collection before completing her studies at the New York School of Interior Design and opening the studio.
