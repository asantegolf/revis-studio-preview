Quality

Rugs that grow more beautiful with use.

Our carpets are made with Tibetan long-staple wool, traditionally hand-knotted in Nepal.

Wool

We use Tibetan long-staple wool, sourced from sheep raised on the high plateaus of the Himalaya. The fibre is hand-spun and hand-carded before it reaches the loom.

Dye

Yarns are batch-dyed by hand, using either natural vegetable pigments or premium Swiss dyes — chosen for the room the carpet will live in.

Weave

Each carpet is traditionally woven by Nepal-based master craftspeople using the Tibetan knot.

Because of the quality of the yarn, the careful dyeing, and the hand-knotting technique, your rugs will only increase in beauty and lustre with use over the years.
