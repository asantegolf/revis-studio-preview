About the studio

A team of skilled craftsmen and expert weavers.

Revis Studio takes a custom carpet from inspiration through design and manufacturing, to final presentation.

The studio

Founded by Toby Revis in 2001, Revis Studio is a custom carpet atelier on Manhattan's Upper East Side. We work with architects, interior designers and private clients on residential and commercial interiors — fine homes, hotels and luxury retail among them.

Each carpet is drawn for a single room and made by hand. We work direct to the source: our weaving partners in Nepal use traditional Tibetan technique, and our standards for fibre, dye, and finish are uncompromising.

Toby Revis

Toby studied painting and architecture at Syracuse University and in Madrid.

She qualified as a certified gemologist at Van Cleef & Arpels, and went on to become a senior buyer at Tiffany & Co. for designer Elsa Peretti.

After completing her studies at the New York School of Interior Design, she managed a custom rug showroom before founding Revis Studio as an independent consultancy in 2001.
