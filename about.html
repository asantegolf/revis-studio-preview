About the studio

A team of skilled craftsmen and expert weavers.

The studio

We specialise in manufacturing for architects & designers.

Revis Studio crafts lush custom carpets for fine hotels, luxury retail, and remarkable homes. Our New York team delivers expert design guidance, uncompromising quality, and exceptional value for residential and corporate interiors.

The founder

Raised midway between Boston and New York City, Toby Revis studied painting and architecture at Syracuse University and in Madrid. She developed a celebrity clientele selling jewels at Van Cleef & Arpels, where she was a certified gemologist, and was later recruited by Tiffany & Co. as a senior buyer for Elsa Peretti. She enrolled in the New York School of Interior Design while managing a custom rug showroom; that fiercely devoted clientele followed her to Revis Studio when she founded it in 2001.
